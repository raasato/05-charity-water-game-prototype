The 6KM Challenge

In this game, you will help a community access clean water by making strategic decisions and managing resources effectively.

How to Play:

  • Make strategic decisions to bring clean water back to your community. Choose your speed by walking or running.
  • Keep an eye on your water levels as you progress.
  • Watch your heat levels to avoid overheating.
  • Receive donations from supporters when you reach milestones to replenish your water supply.
  • Complete the 6KM challenge to win the game and help your community thrive!
